Privacy by Design

Our commitment to privacy as a foundational principle — not a compliance exercise.

Principle

Privacy at Vishpala is not a legal obligation we satisfy at minimum cost. It is a foundational design principle that shapes every decision about what we build, what data we collect, and what infrastructure we use.

We apply the seven foundational principles of Privacy by Design as articulated by Ann Cavoukian:

  1. Proactive, not reactive — preventive, not remedial
  2. Privacy as the default setting
  3. Privacy embedded into design
  4. Full functionality — positive-sum, not zero-sum
  5. End-to-end security — full lifecycle protection
  6. Visibility and transparency
  7. Respect for user privacy — keep it user-centric

What we collect and why

This website — we do not use analytics tracking. We do not set cookies. We do not load third-party scripts. No data about visitors to this site is collected, transmitted to third parties, or retained.

Client engagements — we collect the information necessary to deliver our work: contact information, project materials, and communications. We do not collect information beyond what is needed. We do not retain client data beyond the period required for the engagement and any applicable legal retention requirement.

Communications — email and other communications with us are stored on infrastructure we operate. We do not use email marketing platforms or CRM systems that transmit your contact information to third parties.

Your rights

Under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy legislation, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Access that information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for processing where consent is the legal basis
  • Have your information deleted, subject to legal retention requirements

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@vishpala.com.

Infrastructure

This site is hosted on infrastructure we operate directly. No data is processed by cloud platforms operated by third parties. Our fonts are self-hosted. We do not use content delivery networks operated by third parties for this site.

Contact

Questions about this policy or about how we handle personal information: privacy@vishpala.com.
