## Who we are

Vishpala is a multidisciplinary studio. We are technologists, designers, and infrastructure specialists who work together on problems that require all three disciplines to solve well.

We take our name from the Rigvedic figure Vishpala — a warrior who loses a leg in battle and is given an iron prosthetic by the Ashvins so she can return to the field. We are interested in the idea of prosthetics: tools and infrastructure that extend capability, restore agency, and allow people to do what they came to do.

## What we believe

**Sovereignty matters.** Organisations that depend entirely on infrastructure they do not control are fragile in ways that are not always visible until something breaks. We help organisations understand their dependencies and, where it serves them, reduce them.

**Accessibility is not optional.** We do not build things that exclude people with disabilities, and we do not help other organisations build them. WCAG 2.2 Level AA is our floor on every project.

**Complexity should be proportionate.** The right infrastructure for a community organisation is not the right infrastructure for a national one. We size solutions to the actual operational capacity of the people who will maintain them.

**Excellence takes time.** We work on fewer things at once than most studios. We do not take on work we cannot do well.

## How we work

We work in close collaboration with our clients throughout a project — not in isolation with periodic check-ins. We believe the people who will use and maintain what we build should understand it, which means building understanding as we go rather than handing over a finished system at the end.

We work in English and French.
